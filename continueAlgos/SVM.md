Support Vector Machines (SVM)

SVM is a powerful classifier known for its ability to find the optimal boundary between different groups of data.

The Concept: It searches for the Maximum Margin Hyperplane. Instead of just finding a line that separates two classes, it looks for the "widest possible road" between them to ensure better accuracy on new, unseen data.

The Project: Handwritten Digit Recognition

Goal: Identify handwritten numbers (0-9) from image data.

Implementation: Use the classic MNIST dataset. Each pixel becomes a feature, and the SVM finds the boundaries that best separate the shapes of different digits.
