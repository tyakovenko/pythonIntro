Notes on dataset selection: 
- Majority of the Kaggle datasets are cleaned and normalized meaning that they do not accurately represent the real world topic
- If you need to accurately represent something, collect your own data with strict data collection protocols or use government collected data
- All of the suggested Kaggle data sources for proposed projects in these lessons do not accurately represent the real-world and are only used for practice
